Did You Know?

  • The earliest known frozen pops date back as far as 1872. Back then, a frozen pop was called a Hokey-Pokey.
  • Frozen are also known as freezer pops, ice lollies, ice pops.
  • The world's largest frozen pop was made in 1997 and was 21 feet tall.
  • Frozen pops have become so POP-ular that a common arts and crafts item is called a popsicle stick, named after the most famous brand of pop.
